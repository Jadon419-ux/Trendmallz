Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

TrendMallz ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use our website, services, and features, including account login, shopping tools, AI try-on features, and social login integrations.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, password, country/region, and profile preferences.
  • Order and payment data: cart contents, order history, transaction references, and payment status.
  • Profile content: uploaded photos and avatar data (including optional third-party avatar connections).
  • Usage data: device/browser details, pages visited, and interaction events.
  • Support communications: messages you send to our support channels.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the TrendMallz platform.
  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Process checkouts, payments, and order fulfillment.
  • Power features like referrals, wishlists, dashboards, and AI styling tools.
  • Improve product experience, security, and performance.
  • Communicate service notices and support responses.

3. Social Login and Third-Party Services

If you connect a third-party account (for example, Snapchat for avatar/Bitmoji features), we only receive data made available through the permissions you grant. You can disconnect these integrations at any time from your profile settings.

4. Payments

Payments are handled by third-party payment providers. We do not store full card details on our servers. Payment providers process payment data under their own privacy and security policies.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and local storage to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and improve performance. You can control cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if disabled.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share personal information only as needed with:

  • Service providers that help run our platform (hosting, analytics, authentication, payments).
  • Logistics and fulfillment partners for delivery-related operations.
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity.

We do not sell personal information.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices and Rights

  • Update profile data from your account dashboard.
  • Disconnect social integrations from profile settings.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.

10. Children

TrendMallz is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date above.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at: privacy@trendmallz.com
